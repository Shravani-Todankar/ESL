Implementation of Composite Skills Lab is not just infrastructure. Schools need to synchronise Infrastructure, Curriculum Frameworks, Teacher Capacity, Timetable Integration, Assessment & Documentation, and Annual Showcase (Kaushal Mela). And doing all this while nurturing the joy of learning among children is the real challenge

That's where traditional STEM or Skill Programs fail. CSL is not about compliance or creating mandated projects. It is about building Problem-solving Ability, Developing Design Thinking, Encouraging Entrepreneurial Mindset, and Connecting Skills to Real-world Contexts

ENpower presents a complete, school-wide implementation model for Grades 6-8, it integrates CBSE-aligned infrastructure, the Kaushal Bodh framework, Design Thinking, and Entrepreneurship into 110+ hours of experiential learning, where students work on real-world problems (not just science projects).

ENpower CSL synchronises curriculum, integration with school calendar, teacher capacity building, competency-based assessment, and annual showcases, making it a connected learning system that builds problem-solving ability, confidence, and future readiness among students.
