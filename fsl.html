Why Do
Future Skills
Matter?

The future of work is evolving rapidly. To nurture today's school children as tomorrow's global workforce, school education must move beyond traditional boundaries. Global Skill Frameworks, NEP 2020, and Indian Educational Boards emphasize the need to build competencies in 21st-century life skills, emerging technologies, innovative thinking, and entrepreneurial mindsets, calling for a comprehensive skill framework.
