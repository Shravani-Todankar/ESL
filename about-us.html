ENpower is an education innovation organization working to bridge the growing gap between what schools traditionally teach and what the future of work demands. As careers evolve rapidly, students need more than academic knowledge — they need future-ready skills, mindsets, and real-world capability.

ENpower builds integrated future-skills learning ecosystems that combine experiential skill labs, entrepreneurship education, teacher enablement, and impact-driven partnerships. Anchored in Globally Recognized Frameworks and aligned with NEP 2020 and Indian education boards, ENpower has developed an ecosystem that strengthens mainstream education while ensuring children across diverse contexts are prepared to thrive as thinkers, creators, and problem-solvers in a changing world.

Our Alliance with Tata ClassEdge

Tata ClassEdge has partnered with ENpower (Enlearning Skill Development Limited) to integrate future-focused entrepreneurship and life skills into school education, creating a comprehensive ecosystem for career readiness by blending academic learning with practical, experiential programs like design thinking and problem-solving, supported by technology and teacher training to prepare students for the evolving world. This strategic collaboration involves Tata ClassEdge taking an equity stake in ENpower to scale these programs nationwide, bridging the gap between classroom learning and real-world opportunities.
