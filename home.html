The ENpower Philosophy

The future of work is evolving faster than education. Global frameworks like OECD, WEF, UNICEF, and India's NEP 2020 stress the need for future-skills, yet most schools stop at robotics and coding.

ENpower bridges this gap with a holistic future-skilling ecosystem that builds thinking, entrepreneurship, and life skills. Our 17-skill, 5-outcome model helps students move from learning concepts to creating solutions for tomorrow's world.

About ENpower & TATA ClassEdge Alliance

Tata ClassEdge and ENpower have formed a strategic partnership, announced in December 2025, to integrate entrepreneurship and future-skills education into K-12 schools, with Tata ClassEdge taking an equity stake in ENpower (Enlearning Skill Development Ltd.). This collaboration focuses on delivering experiential learning, life skills, and design thinking through gamified programs, aiming to develop confident, creative, and future-ready children beyond traditional academics, leveraging ENpower's expertise with Tata ClassEdge's digital learning solutions.
