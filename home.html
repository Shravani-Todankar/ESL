The ENpower Philosophy

The future of work is evolving faster than education systems can adapt. Automation, AI, and climate transitions are reshaping careers and yet most students today are still being prepared for jobs that may not exist tomorrow.

Global frameworks by the OECD, World Economic Forum, UNICEF, alongside India's NEP 2020, consistently highlight the urgent need for future-skills education including 21st-century life skills, STEM application, innovative thinking, and entrepreneurial mindset. What's missing is not the intent - it's integration.
